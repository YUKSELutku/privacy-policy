1. Information Collection and Use

The Application does not require you to create a user account, and it does not collect or upload any personal identification information to our servers. All core app functionalities are processed locally on your device.

However, to provide specific features, the Application requests the following permissions and data:

  • Location Data: The Application requires access to your device's location (GPS/Network) to calculate accurate prayer times and determine the Qibla direction based on your current geographical position. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to, stored on, or shared with our servers.
  • Notifications: The Application requires permission to send local notifications to alert you when a prayer time has arrived.
